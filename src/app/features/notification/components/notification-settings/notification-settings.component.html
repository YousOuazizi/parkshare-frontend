About Notification Preferences

Customize how and when you receive notifications from ParkShare. You can control notifications across multiple channels including email, push notifications, and SMS.

  • Email: Detailed updates sent to your registered email address
  • Push: Instant notifications on your mobile device or browser
  • SMS: Text messages for critical updates (carrier rates may apply)

Note: Some critical notifications like security alerts and account changes cannot be disabled.
